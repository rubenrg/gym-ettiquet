Keeping it Classy at the Church of Iron

We're gonna class it up a bit and learn the do's and do not's of proper gym etiquette would you please join us

Do's & Do Not's

  • Put some deodorant on and do your fellow gym goers a favor be sweet not sour.
  • See that good-looking person over there? why don't you go over there and try to start up a conversation with them well better yet DON'T they're here to bust ass and get a good workout in and you should be doing the same thing wait till after.
  • Refrain from using the bench as your personal storage facility believe me the other members will thank you for it.
  • Don't hog all the weights thank you!
  • If you see a bro in trouble help him out who returned the favor and you may have even found yourself a future training match team.

No proper gym etiquette is being aware of others around you and you know what they're all in it for the exact same reason you are making those sweet sweet gains so wipe down your sweat, rear a cure' weights hold your chest up, high back straight and take a moment to recognize the gym gentlemen and women who help keep it classy in the Church of Iron Cheers!
